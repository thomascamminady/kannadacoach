Welcome to the Kannada Learning App!

  • Type the English transliteration of the Kannada word shown
  • Use the spacebar to move to the next character
  • Correct characters turn green
  • Incorrect input shows a red flash
  • After 4 wrong attempts, the correct answer is shown
  • English meaning appears when you complete a word
  • Your transliteration appears below each Kannada letter

Keep practicing to improve your Kannada transliteration skills!
